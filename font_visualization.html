🔴 Serif (Tails): Look at the small "feet" on the 'A' and 'g'. This is what makes it "Serif".
🔵 Sans-Serif (Clean): "Sans" means without. Notice there are no feet or tails. It is very modern.
🟢 Monospace (Fixed Width): Notice that the space taken by 'A' and 'g' is exactly the same. It looks like a typewriter.
